1. Information We Collect

We collect information that you provide directly to us and information that is automatically collected when you use our services.

Information You Provide to Us:

  • Contact Information: Name, email address, phone number, WhatsApp number
  • Professional Information: Current job status, educational background, career goals
  • Application Data: Responses to qualification questions, career challenges, learning preferences
  • Communication Data: Messages, questions, and feedback you send to us
  • Payment Information: Billing details when you enroll in paid programs (processed securely through third-party payment processors)

Information Automatically Collected:

  • Usage Data: Pages visited, time spent on site, interactions with content
  • Device Information: Browser type, operating system, IP address, device identifiers
  • Analytics Data: Website performance metrics, user behavior patterns

🔒 Data Minimization

We only collect information that is necessary for providing our educational services and improving your learning experience. We never collect sensitive personal data unless specifically required.

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

Educational Services:

  • Providing access to our AI & Data learning programs
  • Personalizing your learning experience and curriculum
  • Tracking your progress and providing feedback
  • Facilitating communication with instructors and mentors
  • Organizing live sessions, workshops, and group activities

Communication:

  • Sending course updates, schedules, and important announcements
  • Providing customer support and answering your questions
  • Sending marketing communications (with your consent)
  • WhatsApp notifications for class reminders and updates

Business Operations:

  • Processing payments and managing subscriptions
  • Analyzing website usage to improve our services
  • Preventing fraud and ensuring platform security
  • Compliance with legal obligations

Career Support:

  • Matching you with relevant job opportunities
  • Connecting you with potential clients for freelance work
  • Providing career guidance and placement assistance
  • Building your professional portfolio and profile

3. Information Sharing and Disclosure

We respect your privacy and do not sell your personal information to third parties. We may share your information in the following limited circumstances:

Service Providers:

  • Payment Processors: Secure payment handling (Razorpay, Stripe)
  • Communication Tools: Email service providers, WhatsApp Business API
  • Analytics Services: Google Analytics, Facebook Pixel (anonymized data)
  • Learning Platforms: Course delivery and progress tracking systems

Business Partners:

  • Hiring Partners: With your explicit consent, we may share your profile with potential employers
  • Industry Mentors: Connecting you with industry professionals for guidance
  • Collaboration Partners: Other educational institutions for joint programs

Legal Requirements:

  • Compliance with applicable laws and regulations
  • Response to legal processes, court orders, or government requests
  • Protection of our rights, privacy, safety, or property
  • Investigation of fraud or security issues

⚠️ Your Control

You have complete control over career-related sharing. We will never share your information with potential employers or clients without your explicit, informed consent.

4. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information:

Technical Safeguards:

  • Encryption: All data transmission is encrypted using SSL/TLS
  • Secure Storage: Data stored on secure, access-controlled servers
  • Access Controls: Limited access to personal data on a need-to-know basis
  • Regular Monitoring: Continuous monitoring for security threats and vulnerabilities

Organizational Measures:

  • Staff training on privacy and security practices
  • Regular security audits and assessments
  • Incident response procedures for data breaches
  • Data retention policies to minimize data storage

Data Retention:

  • Active Students: Data retained for the duration of your enrollment plus 2 years
  • Completed Programs: Progress data retained for 5 years for certification purposes
  • Marketing Data: Retained until you opt-out or request deletion
  • Financial Records: Retained as required by applicable tax and accounting laws

5. Your Privacy Rights

You have several rights regarding your personal information:

Access and Portability:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal information we hold about you
  • Portability: Receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format
  • Explanation: Understand how we use your information and who we share it with

Correction and Updates:

  • Correction: Update or correct inaccurate personal information
  • Completion: Add missing information to your profile
  • Profile Management: Update your preferences and settings at any time

Deletion and Restriction:

  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal information (subject to legal requirements)
  • Restriction: Limit how we process your information
  • Objection: Object to certain types of processing (e.g., marketing)

Communication Preferences:

  • Opt-out: Unsubscribe from marketing communications at any time
  • Preference Center: Manage your communication preferences
  • Essential Communications: Some service-related communications cannot be opted out

📧 Exercising Your Rights

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at privacy@skillaipath.com. We will respond to your request within 30 days.

We use cookies and similar technologies to enhance your experience on our website:

Types of Cookies We Use:

  • Essential Cookies: Necessary for website functionality and security
  • Performance Cookies: Help us understand how visitors interact with our website
  • Functional Cookies: Remember your preferences and personalize your experience
  • Marketing Cookies: Show you relevant advertisements and track campaign effectiveness

Third-Party Services:

You can control cookies through your browser settings. However, disabling certain cookies may affect the functionality of our website and your user experience.
