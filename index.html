Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to the most common questions about our real business problem-based learning approach.

How is this different from other AI/Data courses? +

Most courses teach theory with toy datasets. We start with real business problems from actual companies. You learn by solving problems that companies actually pay to solve, not made-up examples. This gives you immediate practical experience and a portfolio that showcases real-world skills.

Do I need prior programming experience? +

No programming experience needed! We teach you everything from scratch, but in the context of solving real problems. You'll learn faster because you'll understand why you're learning each concept and how it applies to real business challenges.

What kind of support do I get during learning? +

Daily live doubt resolution sessions (7-8 PM), WhatsApp community access, personal mentor guidance, and technical setup assistance. Since we work on real problems, you'll get stuck sometimes - that's normal and we're here to help you through it.

How do you ensure job readiness? +

By solving real business problems, you build a portfolio that directly showcases skills employers need. Our students have examples of actual problems they've solved, not just certificates. Plus, you learn to communicate business impact - a crucial skill most courses skip.

What if I can't keep up with the pace? +

Real problems don't have fixed timelines, and neither do we. We provide lifetime access to materials and ongoing support. If you need more time on a particular problem, that's perfectly fine. The goal is mastery, not speed.

Can I really get clients or jobs after this? +

Our approach gives you real project experience that employers and clients value. You'll have concrete examples of business problems you've solved, which is what matters in interviews and client meetings. While we can't guarantee specific outcomes, our method gives you the strongest possible foundation.
